About Company

Find out what separates us from the rest

OpenPath Technologies Integrity, Innovation, Collaboration & Excellence!

OpenPath LLC is a federal and state recognized veteran, woman, and minority owned consulting company based out of Lawrenceville, Georgia in the United States. We are in the business of acquiring and executing short and long term contracts.

Our focus and mission is to accurately guide individuals and companies through the various "paths" that present themselves as their business matures. Our name was founded on the principle that the "open paths" that lie ahead for companies and individuals are incalculable, so having a guide that navigates those different paths beforehand is absolutely invaluable. Our drive is to explore all potential open paths in our customers' purview and present the best solution through our seasoned experiences and skillsets.

Our Mission & Vision

To Build a World-Class Product Engineering & Design Consultancy
for the Best Ideas, Bringing Innovation & Disruption in Industry

Driving value for our clients & partners

OpenPath Technologies works across the entire life cycle of a problem & supports our brand partners through the unique idea curation, design, MVP, product strategy, engineering, deployment, maintenance & support.

We have expert resources in both open source & licensed web technologies, mobile apps, UI/UX design, testing, cloud, infrastructure & marketing to support our clients through innovations & growth.

Our software & systems are running across Apple stores across the US and Europe. These aren’t any obscure components but full-fledged systems used by Apple and their end customers in Apple stores on regular basis.

More than 98% of Company’s clients acclaim our delivery model, reliability, passion, creativity, and unique ability to handle widest range of their IT needs from design, development, marketing to cloud & infrastructure set up, server management, maintenance & support. Company is relentlessly working on the emerging and upcoming technologies to provide market leading solutions for our clients.
