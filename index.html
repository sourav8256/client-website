Mobile Workflow Management

OpenPathLLC Technologies is a World Leader in assisting Telecom Operators, Mobile Buyback Providers, Ecommerce, Online Marketplaces, Refurbished device traders, Phone Repair, Phone Recyclers & Warehouse Providers with sophisticated Mobile Lifecycle Solutions to optimise device operations, increase revenue & maximize customer experience.

Our Mobile Lifecycle Solutions include complete suite of solutions including Make Model Recognition (MMR), Device Diagnostic Testing, Data Wipe or Erase, Buyback or Trade-in Automation, Bulk Inspection, Intelligent Dashboard, Reporting & Custom Device Workflow Management.

We work with our clients to make their processes more agile, secure, cost effective & transparent.
